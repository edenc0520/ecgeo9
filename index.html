What is the issue?

On Monday, November 20th, a landslide was reported to have happened near the city of Wrangell in Alaska. As a result, 4 lives were lost and 2 others are reported to be missing.

Spatial Signifance

Where and Why did it happen?

The landslide occured on mile 11 of the Zimovia Highway. Officials say it is likely the cause of the landslide was due to an atmospheric river swarming over southeastern Alaska a few days before the landslide.

Why should we care?

Although Landslides are uncommon in Urban areas like the GTA, in the future, you may move to an area where landslides are frequent. Understanding what a landslide is, how it occurs, and how to lower the chance of being caught up in one.

Interrelationships

There is a Nature to Nature interrelationship that occurs. Heavy rainfall increases the likelihood of a landslide occurance.
